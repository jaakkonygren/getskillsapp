About Skills

Skills turns calisthenics training videos into on-device analysis and private skill history. It helps you confirm supported skills, review movement with a skeleton overlay, and measure supported reps or hold durations.

Core analysis runs on your device. Skills does not require an account or upload your training videos to a Skills server for analysis. You choose what to save, export, share, or delete.
