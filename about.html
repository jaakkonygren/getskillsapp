If you are reporting an app issue, include your device model, iOS or Android version, Skills app version, and a short description of what happened.

Please do not send training videos, private screenshots, or sensitive personal information unless we ask for specific details needed to investigate your support request.

About Skills

Skills analyzes calisthenics training videos on your device. It detects supported skills, measures repetitions or hold time, shows a skeleton overlay, and saves attempts into your local skill history.

No remote AI processing. No default cloud upload. Your videos and analysis history stay on your device unless you choose to export or share them.
