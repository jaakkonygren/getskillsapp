Calisthenics video analysis

Turn training videos into skill progress.

Import a training video and Skills analyzes it directly on your phone. It detects supported calisthenics skills, shows your movement with a clean skeleton overlay, measures the key result, and saves the attempt into your skill history.

Analyze

Your own attempts

Skills is built for calisthenics practitioners who already record their training and want more than a camera roll full of clips. Use it for handstand holds, L-sits, pull-ups, dips, push-ups, squats, muscle-ups, levers, handstand push-ups, and more.

Measure

What happened

Each analysis gives you a focused view of the attempt: original video preview, skeleton overlay, detected skill, rep count or hold time, analysis confidence, and a saved result in your skill history.

Track

Track privately

One video is useful. A history of attempts is where progress becomes visible. Skills organizes analyzed videos by skill while keeping analysis on-device by default. No remote AI processing. No cloud upload.
