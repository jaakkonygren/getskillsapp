Private calisthenics video analysis

Your training videos should do more than disappear into your camera roll.

Import an attempt and Skills analyzes it on your device. Confirm the skills shown, review the movement with a clean skeleton overlay, and save supported results to your skill history.

Analyze

Start with your own video

Choose a training clip from your device. Skills proposes supported calisthenics skills and lets you confirm or correct them before the final result.

Measure

See what happened

Review the original attempt with a synchronized skeleton overlay and supported rep counts or hold durations. When a clip cannot be measured reliably, Skills can leave it unscored instead of inventing a result.

Track

Build private skill history

Save attempts by skill, revisit your latest and best measured results, and create annotated exports. Core analysis stays on your device. No account is required, and your training videos are not uploaded to a Skills server for analysis.
